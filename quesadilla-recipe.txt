// Quesadillas
Ingredients:
 - Oaxaca Cheese
 - Tortillas (flour or corn)
 - Salsa (optional)


Directions:
 1. Preheat a flat pan on the stovetop until hot.
 
 2. Put as many tortillas as you can fit without overlapping.
 
 3. After the bottom side seems cooked, flip it and add cheese.
 
 4. Fold tortilla in half and make sure both sides look cooked. 
 
 5. Dip in salsa or put salsa in the middle. 
 
 6. Enjoy
